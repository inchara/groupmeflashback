What's this all about!?

There were times when I loved some of the groupme messages that I received, and so did a lot of my friends! These loved messages were those with the most "hearts" and there were parties and times when I wanted to just play all the pictures that were most upvoted, but there was no way to filter out the noise on the unimportant messages. That's when I decided to make groupmePensieve here, just so I could extract groupme messages based on their popularity , by mentioning the minimum and maximum hearts they received, and by pointing out the start date from when the messages need to be collected. I wondered if it would be of any use to the rest of you groupme users, so here it is, on a website!
